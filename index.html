Inclusive Baseball Project

The Inclusive Baseball Project is a PSA website focused on raising awareness about making baseball more accessible for athletes with disabilities. The goal is to highlight the value of inclusive sports, build community support, and encourage people to take action through involvement, advocacy, and donations.

⚾ Why Inclusive Baseball Matters

Baseball creates opportunities for teamwork, confidence, discipline, and community. Athletes with disabilities deserve the same access to those experiences. Inclusive baseball helps open the game to more families and creates a stronger, more welcoming sports culture for everyone.

📘 What This Site Does

This website introduces the issue, explains current programs and opportunities, and gives visitors clear ways to support the mission. Users can learn about programs, view upcoming events, explore resources, read stories, and find ways to get involved.

🤝 Ways to Support the Mission

  • Volunteer at clinics and community events
  • Donate to help expand programs and equipment access
  • Spread awareness through schools, teams, and local organizations
  • Encourage partnerships that support adaptive and inclusive sports
