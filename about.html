About

The Inclusive Baseball Project is centered on the idea that sports should be accessible to everyone. This PSA focuses on raising awareness about athletes with disabilities and the need for more inclusive baseball opportunities in communities, schools, and organized programs.

The purpose of the project is both informational and action-driven. It is meant to educate users about accessibility in sports while also encouraging them to contribute through volunteering, supporting programs, attending events, and sharing the message with others.

By combining strong visuals, simple navigation, and clear messaging, the site is designed to make the topic understandable and approachable. The overall goal is to show that inclusive baseball is not just possible, but valuable for athletes, families, and communities.
