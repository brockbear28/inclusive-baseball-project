Programs

Adaptive Baseball Clinics

Clinics can provide skill instruction, confidence-building, and accessible ways for athletes with disabilities to experience baseball in a supportive environment. These programs can be tailored to different ability levels and help players build both physical skills and social connections.

Community Partnerships

Partnerships with schools, youth leagues, nonprofits, and local organizations can create more opportunities for inclusive events and programming. Collaboration is one of the strongest ways to increase visibility and long-term support.

Special Events and Awareness Programs

Events such as inclusive game nights, clinics, and awareness campaigns help bring the community together. These moments give athletes and families a chance to be seen, supported, and celebrated in a public setting.
